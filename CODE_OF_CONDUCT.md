# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make our community welcoming, safe, and equitable for all.

We are committed to fostering an environment that respects and promotes the
dignity, rights, and contributions of all individuals, regardless of
characteristics including race, ethnicity, caste, color, age, disability,
neurodiversity, sex or gender, gender identity or expression, sexual orientation,
language, philosophy or religion, national or social origin, socio-economic
position, level of education, or other status.

## Encouraged Behaviors

1. Respecting the purpose of our community and the ways we collaborate.
2. Engaging kindly and honestly with others.
3. Respecting different viewpoints and experiences.
4. Gracefully giving and accepting constructive feedback.
5. Taking responsibility for our actions and committing to repairing harm when it occurs.

## Restricted Behaviors

The following behaviors are violations of this Code of Conduct:

1. **Harassment.** Violating expressed boundaries or continuing unwanted personal attention after a request to stop.
2. **Character attacks.** Insulting, demeaning, or pejorative comments directed at a community member.
3. **Discrimination or stereotyping.** Characterizing anyone based on immutable identities or traits.
4. **Sexualization.** Behavior that would be considered inappropriately intimate in a community context.
5. **Violating confidentiality.** Sharing someone's personal information without their permission.
6. **Endangerment.** Causing, encouraging, or threatening violence or harm toward any person.

## Reporting

To report a possible violation, email **joe@retireeplan.app**. All reports will
be reviewed and investigated promptly and with confidentiality.

## Addressing Harm

When a violation is found, the following ladder guides the response:

1. **Warning** — A private written warning and request for repair (e.g. apology, acknowledgement).
2. **Temporary limits** — A cooldown period with limited participation in specific channels.
3. **Temporary suspension** — Suspension with conditions for return.
4. **Permanent ban** — Removal from all community spaces, reserved for serious or repeated violations.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces and when an individual
officially represents the community in public spaces.
